Research Experience

Xtra Computing Group, National University of Singapore Jun - Sep 2019
Research Intern, supervised by Prof. Bingsheng He and Dr. Zeyi Wen.

In order to explain some unexplainable models in artificial intelligence, we designed a system which uses simple machine learning models such as weighted k-means to approximate the unexplainable models. Our system supports 4 interpretation methods including Prototype, Feature influence, Feature contribution and Counterfactual explaination. The result of our work will be submitted to KDD 2020.
