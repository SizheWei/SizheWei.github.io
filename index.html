Xtra Computing Group, National University of Singapore
Research Intern, supervised by Dr. Bingsheng He.
Jun - Sep 2019
In order to explain some unexplainable models in artificial intelligence, we designed a system which uses simple machine learning models such as weighted k-means to approximate the unexplainable models.
Our system supports 4 interpretation methods including Prototype, Feature influence, Feature contribution and Counterfactual explaination.
The result of our work will be submitted to KDD 2020.
